About Us

Who We Are

The Pigeon Institute of Robotics at Ahmedabad was established in 2018 to conduct basic and applied learning in robotics technologies. Seeking to combine the practical and the theoretical knowledge in childrens, the Robotics Institute has diversified its efforts and approaches to robotics science while retaining its original goal of realizing the potential of the robotics field. We are proud of the open, friendly culture to the childrens, to explore robotics and apply its creativity in robotics field and create a better future for themself.
